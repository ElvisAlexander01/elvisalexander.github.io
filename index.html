A letter

From me, to you.

Dear Stephanie,

I know your mind is made up, and I want you to know — I respect that. I'm not writing this to pull you back, or to make you feel guilty, or to ask you for anything.

I'm writing this because some feelings deserve to be said out loud, even if no one answers.

I loved you. I still do. Quietly, in the small hours when no one is watching. In songs I can't listen to anymore. In photos I scroll past too quickly. In the silence where your voice used to be.

I'm sorry for the misunderstanding — for the part of it that was mine. I would undo it a thousand times if I could. But I can't, and I've learned to live with that.

If life takes you far from me, I hope it takes you somewhere beautiful. I hope someone loves you the way you deserve — patiently, gently, completely. I hope you laugh that laugh I used to live for. I hope you're happy, Stephanie. Truly.

And if one day — only if — your heart ever turns this way again, you'll find me here. Not waiting in a way that traps you. Just… here. Still soft. Still yours, in the quiet way I've always been.

Thank you for every moment. Even the ones that hurt. They were yours, and that made them worth it.

Always,
— Me
